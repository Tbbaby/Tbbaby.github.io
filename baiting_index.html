About Me

Ting Bai

baiting AT ruc.edu.cn

I am a PhD student in School of Information, Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Ji-Rong Wen and Prof. Xin Zhao . My major research interests are in Recommender Systems, Deep Learning and Data Mining. I am now a Visiting Research Student at University of Montreal, Canada, supervised by Prof. Jian-Yun Nie . I have published several papers as the First Author on SIGIR, CIKM, TKDE, JCST and so on, two of them won the Best Paper Runner Up Awards on CIKM'17 and Big Data'15 respectively. And I was invited as Reviewers of TKDE and JCST. I will get my PhD degree from Renmin University of China in June of 2019, so I am now looking for a research job in companies or universities.
