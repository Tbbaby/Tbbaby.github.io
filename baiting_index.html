About Me

Ting Bai

baiting AT bupt.edu.cn

I am now an Assistant Professor in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. I got my PhD degree in Renmin University of China in July 2019, supervised by Prof. Ji-Rong Wen and Prof. Xin Zhao . My major research interests are in Recommender Systems, Deep Learning and Data Mining. I had been a Visiting Research Student at University of Montreal, Canada, supervised by Prof. Jian-Yun Nie . I have published several papers as the First Author on SIGIR, KDD, CIKM, WSDM, TKDE, NAACL, JCST and so on, three of them won the Best Paper/ Best Paper Runner Up Awards on KDD'19 workshop, CIKM'17 and Big Data'15 respectively. And I was invited as Reviewers of TKDE,JCST, AAAI, AIRS.
